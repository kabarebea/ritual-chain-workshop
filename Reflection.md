# Reflection: Oracle-Based Verification in Bounties

Oracle-based verification decentralizes the judging process by using multiple independent validators. This eliminates single points of failure and reduces bias.

**Public vs Hidden:** Commitments and oracle votes are public. Answers stay hidden until reveal. Oracle slashing is transparent.

**AI vs Human:** Oracles can be AI agents, humans, or a combination. The consensus mechanism ensures robustness.

**Advantages:** Decentralized, robust against individual bias, staking ensures accountability.

**Trade-off:** Requires multiple oracles and staking mechanism, adds complexity.
